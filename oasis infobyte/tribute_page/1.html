Sundar Pichai

1972 - Present

Sundar Pichai is an Indian-American business executive and the CEO of Alphabet Inc. He was born on July 12, 1972, in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India. Pichai joined Google in 2004 and has played a crucial role in the development of various products and services. Under his leadership, Google launched the Chrome web browser, which quickly became one of the most popular browsers in the world. He also oversaw the development of Android, the operating system that now powers the majority of smartphones globally. In 2015, Pichai was named the CEO of Google Inc., and later, he became the CEO of Alphabet Inc. His vision and leadership have been instrumental in driving the company's growth and expansion into various new areas. Sundar Pichai is widely recognized as one of the most influential and successful tech executives in the world.

Biographies

  • Born on July 12, 1972, in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India.
  • Earned a Bachelor's degree in Metallurgical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur.
  • Pursued further studies in the United States and obtained a Master's degree from Stanford University and an MBA from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.
  • Joined Google in 2004, initially working on the development of the Google Toolbar and Google Chrome browser.
  • Instrumental in the success of Google Chrome, which became one of the most popular web browsers globally.
  • Led the product management and innovation efforts for various Google products, including Google Drive, Google Maps, and Android.
  • Promoted to Senior Vice President of Chrome and Apps, overseeing Google's suite of apps like Gmail, Calendar, and Docs.
  • In 2014, became the product chief, in charge of all Google products, including Android, Chrome, YouTube, and Search. Appointed as CEO of Google Inc. in August 2015, succeeding Larry Page. Under his leadership, Google experienced significant growth and expansion in various areas, including artificial intelligence and cloud computing.
  • In December 2019, Pichai became the CEO of Alphabet Inc., Google's parent company, following a corporate restructuring.
